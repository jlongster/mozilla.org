Bezilla: Contact
Bezilla.org
In order to provide a more focussed and moderated feedback and discussion site, Paul has set up BeZilla.org.
 
Newsgroup
In order to reach us through the newsgroup go download a news reader for BeOS and then point it on news.mozilla.org. Subscribe to the netscape.public.mozilla.beos.
 
Mailing List
On BeOS it may be easier to actually use mail. A mailing list is actually setup for doing that. You can send mail to us but won't be able to read it unless you subscribe to it. You can always unsubscribe to stop receiving the emails.
  • To subscribe click here and press send inside your email application.
  • To post to the mailing list, send your mail to mozilla-beos@mozilla.org.
  • To unsubscribe click here and press send inside your email application.
 
IRC
Some of us, like myself, are passing some time over IRC. So you may find us on #bezilla on irc.mozilla.org
 
Bug Reports
If you have found a bug you can submit it through http://bugzilla.mozilla.org, please be sure to mark the bug as specific to the BeOS. Please follow the bugzilla guidelines when submitting bugs.
