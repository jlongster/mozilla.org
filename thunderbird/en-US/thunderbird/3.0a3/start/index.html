Submit Crash Reports

Please use the Crash Reporter and turn it on when it asks. The Crash Reporter gives us really valuable data on which crashes are the most serious, and how often people encounter them. Click 'OK' when the Crash Reporter prompts you to send feedback.

Join the QA Team! There is a lot to do and we rely on volunteers to keep Thunderbird's high quality.
