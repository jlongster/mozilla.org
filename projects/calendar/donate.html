Lightning is a popular calendaring, scheduling and task management extension. Mozilla Sunbird® is a cross-platform application that brings Mozilla-style ease-of-use to your calendar.

If you enjoy using our applications (Lightning or Mozilla Sunbird®), please consider making a donation. All donations can be made using a credit card, PayPal account or check. The Mozilla Foundation will use your donation to fund activities related to the Calendar Project*.

Tax Exempt Status

The Mozilla Foundation is a California non-profit corporation exempt from federal income taxation under IRC 501(c)3. Donations are tax deductible for U.S. citizens. For donors outside of the United States, please consult with your tax advisor about whether your donation will be tax deductible.
