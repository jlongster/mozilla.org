Test Case Description for  Color and Contrast

Purpose: Verify that there are at least three color combinations that will provide a high contrast color scheme.


Initial  Conditions:
  1. The software allows  users adjust screen colors.
 

Steps/Description:
  1. Change the color selection in the software preference.

 
Expected Results:
  1. Test the application color selections to ensure that there are at least three combinations that will provide a high contrast color scheme. Examples of high contrast color schemes are white on black, black on white, and yellow on blue.

    Lighthouse International provides guidelines for effective color contrast for yorr reference.
