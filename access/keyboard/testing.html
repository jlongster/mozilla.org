Known Mozilla issues

  1. Cannot tab into frames and iframes.
  2. Cannot customize the sidebar with the keyboard only
  3. When focused on a plugin, cannot tab out or use any Mozilla keyboard accelerators
  4. When there's no plugin, "Click here to get plugin" shows in it's place, but that is not keyboard accessible
