This grant offers support to Open Video Alliance activities in support of the open video movement.

Grant Summary

  • 2009: $30,000
  • Focus:Community
  • Recipient: Open Video Alliance
  • Location: United States

More Information

This grant is funding various efforts in the open video movement, such as the operations of openvideoalliance.org, the branding of open video products, outreach to the public media, fundraising and video production.

Open Video Alliance

Open Video Alliance is a coalition of organizations and individuals committed to the idea that the power of moving image should belong to everyone. OVA is a framework to help incubate new projects and campaigns. Core OVA members like Mozilla, PCF, and Kaltura are actively building this future with free and open source software for video—Firefox, Miro, and the Kaltura platform. Other OVA members lead research efforts into how to ensure an open video ecosystem.

Please consider making a contribution to the Mozilla Foundation so we can continue to make grants to other worthwhile organizations such as the Open Video Alliance.
