For two years Mozilla has been a sponsor of the Personal Democracy Forum, an event that creates a forum for discussion on the way that politics and technology intersect.

Grant Summary

  • 2009: $5,000
  • 2008: $10,000
  • Focus: Events
  • Recipient: Personal Democracy Forum
  • Location: United States

More Information

Mozilla was a sponsor of the 2008 Personal Democracy Forum, with Mozilla Foundation board member Brian Behlendorf offering a speech on open source. Mozilla renewed its support as a sponsor for the 2009 conference.

Personal Democracy Forum

The Personal Democracy Forum is an annual conference on the intersection of technology and politics.Each year top opinion makers, political practitioners, technologists and journalists come together to network, exchange ideas, and explore how technology and the Internet are changing politics, democracy, and society.

Please consider making a contribution to the Mozilla Foundation so we can continue to make grants to other worthwhile organizations such as the Personal Democracy Forum.
