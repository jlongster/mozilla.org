This grant, issued in 2009, enabled the creation of an online course called Open|Web|Content|Education -- a six week online course where educators learned about open content licensing, open web technologies and open teaching methods.

Grant Summary

  • 2009: $2,500
  • Focus: Education
  • Recipient: Phillip Schmidt (P2PU)
  • Location: South Africa

More Information

Course topics included open educational resources, open web technology, open content licensing, coming innovations, and approaches in participatory online learning. The course format was a weekly web seminar, with assigments completed both individually and in groups.

Peer2Peer University

P2PU combines open educational resources, structured courses, and recognition of knowledge/learning in order to offer high-quality low-cost education opportunities. It is run and governed by volunteers.
