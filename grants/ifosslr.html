This grant is funding the launch of the International Free and Open Source Software Law Review (IFOSSLR), a collaborative legal publication aiming to increase knowledge and understanding among lawyers about Free and Open Source Software issues.

Grant Summary

  • 2009: $10,000
  • Focus: Community
  • Recipient: IFOSSLR
  • Location: Europe

More Information

This $10,000 grant funded printing of a few thousand copies of the journal for the initial 2-4 issues, as part of the launch of the journal. IFOSSLR is currently receiving some financial and administrative support from the NLnet Foundation, a Dutch nonprofit organization that supports FSF Europe and a number of FOSS projects; a British legal firm has also committed to cover some expenses associated with the journal's launch event.

FFKP

The International Free and Open Source Software Law Review is a collaborative legal publication aiming to increase knowledge and understanding among lawyers about Free and Open Source Software issues. Topics covered include copyright, licence implementation, licence interpretation, software patents, open standards, case law and statutory changes.
