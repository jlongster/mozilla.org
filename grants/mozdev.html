This grant provides general funds to support the operations of MozDev Community Organization. Since 2006, Mozilla grants have funded the majority of MozDev's budget. This support gives back to the community that contributes so much to establishing Mozilla as a viable application development platform and the community that builds quality applications and extensions.

Grant Summary

  • 2008: $30,000
  • 2007: $30,000
  • 2006: $30,000
  • Focus: Community
  • Recipient: MozDev
  • Location: United States

More Information

Mozilla's grant to MozDev in 2006 allowed this community group to hire a full-time staff to improve the features of the mozdev.org site. Mozilla's subsequent grants in 2007 and 2008 allowed further enhancements to mozdev.org and funding other full-time employees.

MozDev

MozDev is a software development community dedicated to making quality applications and extensions freely available to all computer users. Its goal is to help establish Mozilla as a viable application development platform. Free project hosting and software development tools are available to anyone with an idea that is related to Mozilla, helping to increase the value of open source software.

Please consider making a contribution to the Mozilla Foundation so we can continue to make grants to other worthwhile organizations such as MozDev.
