This grant provides sponsorship for a free culture and software conference in Sweden. The Free Society Conference and Nordic Summit was held November 13-15, 2009, in Göteborg Sweden.

Grant Summary

  • 2009: $1,300
  • Focus: Events
  • Recipient: FFKP
  • Location: Sweden

More Information

This is the third year of the FSCONS conference. FSCONS is jointly organized by Free Software Foundation Europe, Creative Commons, and Wikipedia Sverige. Mozilla has not previously sponsored this conference.

FFKP

Föreningen fri kultur och programvara (in English, the Foundation for Free Culture and Software) exists to spread awareness about free culture and free software, as well as support organisations, individuals and projects with the same goals..

Please consider making a contribution to Mozilla so we can continue to make grants to other worthwhile organizations such as FFKP.
