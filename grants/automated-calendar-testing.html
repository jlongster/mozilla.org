This grant is funding the development of calendar automated testing for the Mozilla calendar code.

Grant Summary

  • 2009: $4,500
  • Focus: Open Source
  • Recipient: Merike Sell
  • Location: Estonia

More Information

This was originally an idea presented at the 2009 Google Summer of Code, and Mozilla Calendar developers became interested in funding technology that would enable automated testing.

Merike Sell

Merike Sell is an active member of the Mozilla developer and localization communites who live in Estonia.

Please consider making a contribution to Mozilla so we can continue to make grants to other worthwhile individuals like Merike Sell.
