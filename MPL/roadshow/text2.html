Key Requirements of an Open Source License

  • Source code must be available

  • To anyone, for any purpose, free of charge

    • No field of use limits
      • e.g., requirements to use with a specified processor, operating system, or for a specific function are not permitted
  • No limits on technical decision making
    • e.g., compatibility requirements not permitted
    • e.g., maintenance of specified APIs not permitted
  • No limits on competitive or other business decisions
    • e.g., prohibition on competing with author not permitted
    • e.g., prohibition of “commercial” use not permitted

  • Right to “fork” is required
